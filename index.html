Our Profile

"Experience the World's Favorite Chicken at KFC! Founded by Colonel Harland Sanders in 1952, KFC's secret blend of 11 herbs and spices creates our iconic fried chicken loved globally. With thousands of locations in over 145 countries, we're serving up more than just chicken – our menu features sandwiches, wraps, salads, and sides like mashed potatoes and coleslaw. Proudly part of the Yum! Brands family, our innovative menu items and marketing campaigns make us a cultural icon, shaping both the fast-food industry and popular culture. Join us in our commitment to making a difference through philanthropy, partnering with organizations like the World Food Programme to fight hunger worldwide. Come taste the tradition, the flavor, the legacy – only at KFC!"
